# Italian Word Games Roadmap

Prioritized by popularity/monetization potential.

## Tier 1: High Viral Potential + Strong Monetization

### 1. Parole Crociate (Crossword Puzzle)
**Why:** Universally popular, high engagement time (10-30 min), premium content model proven.
**Monetization:**
- Daily free puzzle + premium archive access ($4.99/mo)
- Difficulty tiers (easy/medium/hard)
- Themed packs (travel, food, history)
**Tech:** Grid-based, clue system, hint coins
**Audience:** 35-65, broad appeal

### 2. Impiccato Sociale (Social Hangman)
**Why:** Multiplayer = retention, simple mechanics, quick rounds (2-3 min)
**Monetization:**
- Lives system (5 free/day, $0.99 for 20 lives)
- Custom word pack creation ($1.99)
- Remove ads ($2.99)
**Tech:** Real-time multiplayer, ELO ranking, friend challenges
**Audience:** 18-45, competitive players

### 3. Paroliere (Italian Boggle)
**Why:** Time-pressure creates urgency, daily leaderboards drive retention
**Monetization:**
- Extra rounds ($0.99 for 10)
- Power-ups (double points, time freeze - $0.49 each)
- Tournament entry fees ($2.99, prize pool split)
**Tech:** 4x4 grid, 3-minute timer, word validation
**Audience:** 25-50, puzzle enthusiasts

## Tier 2: Solid Engagement + Moderate Monetization

### 4. Indovina la Frase (Phrase Builder)
**Why:** Cultural knowledge angle, shareable results, educational value
**Monetization:**
- Premium phrase packs (idioms, proverbs, movie quotes - $1.99)
- Daily challenges with rewards
- Remove ads ($2.99)
**Tech:** Letter reveal mechanic, category system, hint system
**Audience:** 30-60, culturally engaged

### 5. Ruzzle Clone (Italian Word Battle)
**Why:** Proven success (Ruzzle huge in Italy), competitive multiplayer
**Monetization:**
- Lives/energy system
- Power-ups (swap letters, extend time - $0.99)
- Premium stats/badges ($3.99)
**Tech:** Turn-based multiplayer, 2-minute rounds, chain scoring
**Audience:** 20-45, mobile gamers

### 6. Anagrammi Veloci (Speed Anagrams)
**Why:** Quick gameplay loop, infinite replayability, skill progression
**Monetization:**
- Difficulty packs (5/6/7/8 letter words - $0.99 each)
- Remove timer ($1.99 one-time)
- Hints (50 hints for $0.99)
**Tech:** Timer pressure, scoring multipliers, streak system
**Audience:** 25-50, word game fans

## Tier 3: Niche Appeal + Lower Monetization

### 7. Rebus Italiani (Picture Puzzles)
**Why:** Visual appeal, shareable on social media, educational
**Monetization:**
- Premium puzzle packs ($1.99)
- Skip puzzle (10 skips for $0.99)
**Tech:** Image-based clues, difficulty progression
**Audience:** 30-55, casual players

### 8. Trova la Parola (Word Search)
**Why:** Relaxing gameplay, low barrier to entry, therapeutic
**Monetization:**
- Themed collections ($0.99)
- Unlimited hints ($2.99)
- Remove ads ($2.99)
**Tech:** Grid generation, category themes, timer optional
**Audience:** 40-70, stress relief seekers

### 9. Sciarada (Charades/Taboo)
**Why:** Party game potential, in-person multiplayer, viral moments
**Monetization:**
- Premium card decks ($1.99)
- Team tournaments ($4.99)
**Tech:** Card deck system, timer, scoring, video recording option
**Audience:** 18-35, social groups

### 10. Sillabe Impazzite (Syllable Scramble)
**Why:** Unique mechanic, educational for learners, moderate complexity
**Monetization:**
- Learning mode ($1.99)
- Timed challenges
**Tech:** Syllable database, drag-drop interface
**Audience:** 15-30, students/learners

## Tier 4: Experimental/Long-term

### 11. Dialetti d'Italia (Dialect Game)
**Why:** Regional pride, educational, cultural preservation
**Monetization:** Regional packs ($1.99), limited but passionate audience
**Tech:** Audio clips, region selector, translation system
**Audience:** 30-70, regional identity strong

### 12. Proverbi Perduti (Proverb Completion)
**Why:** Educational, generational appeal, nostalgia factor
**Monetization:** Premium proverb collections ($0.99)
**Tech:** Fill-in-blank, multiple choice, difficulty tiers
**Audience:** 45+, traditional values

## Implementation Priority

**Phase 1 (Q1 2026):** Parole Crociate - highest ROI, proven monetization
**Phase 2 (Q2 2026):** Impiccato Sociale - multiplayer drives retention
**Phase 3 (Q3 2026):** Paroliere - capitalize on summer mobile usage
**Phase 4 (Q4 2026):** Evaluate top 3 performance, choose next 2 from Tier 2

## Key Success Metrics

- DAU/MAU > 0.25 (retention)
- Session length > 8 minutes
- D7 retention > 30%
- ARPDAU > $0.05
- Viral coefficient > 1.2 (social sharing)

## Monetization Strategy Notes

**Avoid:** Intrusive ads, pay-to-win mechanics, aggressive paywalls
**Prioritize:**
- Freemium with generous free tier
- Cosmetic/convenience purchases
- Optional premium content (not required for core gameplay)
- Social sharing incentives (reward viral behavior)

## Technical Considerations

- PWA-first (installable, offline-capable)
- i18n from day one (Italian + English at minimum)
- Shared component library across games
- Unified user accounts/progress
- Analytics: Plausible/Fathom (privacy-focused)
